FMVA Financial Modeling Portfolio
A collection of financial models and analysis dashboards completed as part of the Financial Modeling & Valuation Analyst (FMVA) certification program by the Corporate Finance Institute (CFI). Each model was built in Excel and demonstrates a distinct area of financial analysis — from integrated 3-statement modeling and DCF valuation to benchmarking, budgeting, and sensitivity analysis.

6. Sensitivity Analysis
Tornado chart quantifying the impact of key model assumptions on implied share price to identify which drivers carry the most valuation risk.
Show Image
Key Finding: Revenue is by far the most sensitive assumption with a ±~55% impact on share price. Discount Rate has the smallest impact, highlighting that top-line growth assumptions require the most scrutiny in this valuation.
